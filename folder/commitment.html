Commitment and Partnership

Partnership Equality:

Partnership Equality means when both mother and father contribute their roles in responsible for caring the fairly and equal, so that each of them could become equal partners as a couples. In Successful Marriages and Families: Proclamation Principles and Research Perspectives chp4. It emphasize that the "fathers are to preside over their families in love and righteousness and are responsible to provide the necessities of life and protection for their families. And Mothers are primarily responsible for the nurture of their children. In these sacred responsibilities, fathers and mothers are obligated to help one another as equal partners" (Hudson & Miller, ch4).

Moreover, in my opinion having an open communication can let the couples determine their roles that they both need to work on as applying parenting roles when raising their childrens. For example, when mother and father are committed to working together on common family goals to set a great example of supporting the child's life development. Therefore, parents commitment is require for establishing equal parenting in family such as, sharing the responsibilities, duties, and chores of parenting equally between them. This will help them see appreciation and acknowledge each other doings and meaningful contribution to the family and also to make them feel satisfy one another.

Strengthening Families

In the chapter 29 The Proclamation: A Guide, a Banner, and a Doctrinal Summary of the Church's Emphasis on the Family by Lloyd D. Newell. This chapter mentioned so many way couples can do to increase intimacy, passion, and commitment in family. It also explained the role of couples in family as an equal partnership that I categorized it in intimacy, passion, and commitment. Intimacy means in proclamation is when two people have close bond and share feelings for each other. Passion means something you would love to do constantly or defined by both couples have deep feelings of emotion and strong sexual attraction of the things they love to do. And in committing is when two couples active every time in their couples activities, decision making, and family management. But this chapter 29, encourage parents to commit in teaching their children about the family proclamation to build strong happy family.

Overcome Challanges

In the family proclamation The Warm, Happy Marriage: Cold, Hard Facts to Consider by VanDenBerghe and Hawkin. this chapter has provided so much information to couples roles to do when it comes to challanges and problem solving. Also it explain the benefits of the marriage. As I study this proclamation, I have come across solutions for these top issues in family, financial issue, child care issues, and lack of communication. Hawkwins provided some guidelines according to these issues. In Hawkwins research that "married people are generally happier, greater life satisfaction, lower risk for depression, and greater economic stability, all these contributing to better mental health" (Scott et al., 2009; Stack & Eshleman, 1998; Wood, Goesling, & Avellar, 2007). He emphasize that couples synergy like when two people contribution greater together than they when independently. And this would known as equal in making decision, responsability, caring for their children. Having an equal partnership could help couples to solve problem easily and create a better environment with their family as they develop. Therefore, challanges could also help couples to strengthen their parenting role and figure out new things with new ideas to improve their family. (VanDenBerghe & Hawkin, ch.7).
