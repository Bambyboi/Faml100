Financial Disagreements

This chapter has provide so much benefits of being married. Regarding financial issues, “Committed couples hunker down and stay the course together,” and “Although they may experience pain, they may also know the great joy of overcoming challenges and loss together” (Stanley, 2005, p. 200). This quote says that couples need not go straight to the conclusion of getting a divorce or separation. Still, they need open communication about sharing equal thoughts and opinions in their rooms, to find ways to solve financial issues or any other problems. Experiencing pain can help couples think about sharpening their decision-making according to the problem. This chapter mentioned that married adults are healthier than non-married adults and they have a lot of benefits like they will have low rates of morbidity and mortality and their health status persist like couples will bond together and fix past and future problems (Hawkwin, ch7, p.71). Economist has reported that “Married couples, even those with lower incomes, report greater financial security and, as a result, have greater access to better housing, food, and services like health care than the never-married, divorced, or widowed” (Stack & Eshleman, 1998). This means that couples with equally shared values, opinions, and open communication can achieve benefits that were mentioned by the Economist.

In the gospel library, I came across a researcher about an Eternal perspective on personal and family finance (Sudweeks & Hill, 2016). I found some of the spiritual and physical solutions that couples should stay together and discuss to avoid this kind of financial issue. Firstly, pay the Lord first in tithes and offerings. Paying the Lord's tithes and offerings will bless us. In Malachi 3:10, it says that God will open the windows of heaven, and pour blessings to those who pay tithes and fast offerings. Secondly, create and use a budget for monthly planning. Couples should have financial plans and goals like tithing, investments, food, housing, clothing, insurance, medical expenses, and cars. And other annual expenses like Christmas, vacations, and family matters. Having these would also help couples to examine to not spend too much on useless things. Last but not least, avoid debts, if you have debts you need to follow the debt elimination plan below to avoid debts. President Gordon B. Hinckley (191-2008) urged Church members: “Be modest in your expenditures; discipline yourselves in your purchases to avoid debt to the extent possible. Pay off debt as quickly as you can, and free yourselves from bondage.”

Childcare Issues

In this chapter it shared a significant advantages of marriage offers in the realm of child-rearing. A stable marriage like parents honoring marriage vow, having equally partnership, good parenting, support family goals, and provide for their children. Benefit of marriage, let couple experience the parenting role which is one most key point for this issue. Hawkwins and VanDenBerghe ephasize that "A stable marriage corresponded with happiness, health, prosperity, and well-being of offspring and communities" (VanDenBerghe & Hawkin, p.71). Having supportive parents could help the children follow the same example as their parent's parenting them. Such as supporting in shared responsability and mutual support equitably in family could help to build a great environment for raising children, in the proclamation "benefits of Married couples' children, whether black, white, or Hispanic, enjoy better physical health and longevity. The causes lie in better education, healthier behaviors, and more stable economic conditions associated with marriage" (VanDenBerghe & Hawkin, p.74), this quote said that parents need to have better education, and financial management could help them to provide for their children. Benefits in marriage reduce depressive in both men and women mental health and help couples to having support to the theory that marriage is associated with well-being and happy feelings. Being divorce through unequal responsability affects the children development and as a bad example to them, because they will likely do the same thing as they grow up. This chapter said that "people enter and exit marriage affects human behavior such as smoking, diet, exercise, and access to health care" (VanDenBerghe & Hawkin, p.74). These affections will also be on childrens, because when parents separates, the parenting role will not be fully established in family.

In the Mother as Nurturer (Erickson, ch12, p.136), not only mother but father must spend so much time with their young children like age 1-4. "importance of mothers spending time with their children, but they also indicate that it is the way a mother interacts with her children when she is with them that is most important" and "further, childcare that was more like a nurturing home than a childcare center, with a higher adult-to-child ratio that allowed children to receive consistent, responsive nurturing, was not associated with negative effects over the long term" (Erickson, p.137). Therefore, parents must actively need to spend much time with their children as they're in child-to-adult by giving them attentions, talk to them in lower voice as a great parenting, solve their problems, support their education, and keep nurturing them till they become adult and be self-reliance. “When a child is born to a husband and wife, they are fulfilling part of our Heavenly Father's plan to bring children to earth,” Elder Andersen said. In Moses 1:39 The Lord said, "This is my work and my glory—to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man." Therefore, children in God's view, is part of his plan of happiness. Parents' need to take care of them till they have their own.

Lack of communication

In order to improve couples marriage and family life, couples must need to overcome lack of communication (Chapter 7 of "The Warm, Happy Marriage: Cold, Hard Facts to Consider"). "The cornerstone of a strong marriage is not just communication, but the right kind of communication" (VanDenBerghe & Hawkins, 2023, p. 73). Communication is not something that we think lighter on during couples discussion. Hawkwins said, couple need have a deeply conversation, like including honest, openess, express emotions, empathy, willing to provide ideas, and focus. All these aspects will help them to fix the childcare and financial issues and other different kind problems to build their relationship everlasting and make great environment in their home.

I will like to share a story about a husband and a wife miscommunication. So one night they got into a fight, which leading them not to talk with one another silence in long period of time in that night. Even when they prepare for bed. The husband was worried that he need someone to wake him up early morning (6.00am), to catch an early job interview around 8.00am. Therefore, he wrote a letter and stick it on her wife's pillow. Please wake me up around 6:00am. When the husband woke up and he look at the time it's 9.00am in the morning. He was mad and angry and about to have an another argument with her. As he looks at his wife's pillow, to see if the note is still there. He saw a respond of his wife on the note, get up! it's 6.00am. Therefore, this story shows the lack of communication that led him missed his job interview. Always remember that "It is important to fixed the problem right away rather than procrastinate it."
